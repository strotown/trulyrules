OFFICIAL RULES

TRU BY HILTON® TRULY UNIQUE SUMMER CONTEST

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN THIS CONTEST. VOID WHERE PROHIBITED. MUST BE 21 YEARS OF AGE OR OLDER TO ENTER.

This Contest is in no way sponsored, endorsed or administered by, or associated with, Facebook, Instagram or any other social network or platform. You understand that you are providing your information to Contest Entities, and not to Facebook, Instagram or other social network or platform.

ELIGIBILITY

The Contest is open only to legal residents of the United States (excluding Rhode Island) who are at least 21 years old at the time of entry. Employees of Contest Entities, their immediate family members, and persons living in their households are not eligible to enter or win.

2. CONTEST PERIOD

The Contest begins on May 1, 2023, at 12:00 PM Eastern Time (ET) and ends on August 31, 2023, at 11:59 PM ET. All entries must be received by August 31, 2023, at 11:59 PM ET.

3. HOW TO ENTER

  1. Visit the Contest website at www.trulyuniquesummer.com and follow the instructions to complete the entry form with your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and any other required information.
  2. Submit a photograph and a brief description (no more than 250 words) of your truly unique summer experience. The photograph and description must be original, and you must have permission to use any people or property depicted in the photograph.

4. JUDGING AND WINNER SELECTION

On or about September 7, 2023, a panel of qualified judges will select one (1) potential winner from among all eligible entries received. The potential winner will be notified by email or phone within approximately five (5) days of the selection.

5. PRIZES

The winner will receive a travel voucher valued at $5,000, redeemable for travel-related expenses, such as airfare, accommodations, and activities. The voucher must be used within one (1) year of the date of issuance. No cash or other substitution, assignment, or transfer of the prize is permitted, except by Contest Entities, who reserve the right to substitute a prize
